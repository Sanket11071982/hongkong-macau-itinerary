6 Nights / 7 Days Itinerary

Hong Kong & Macau

Day 1: Arrival in Hong Kong

• Arrive at Hong Kong International Airport
• Transfer to hotel and check-in
• Relax and explore nearby local markets (Ladies Market, Temple Street)

Day 2: Hong Kong City Tour

• Visit Victoria Peak (Peak Tram & Sky Terrace)
• Madame Tussauds Wax Museum
• Aberdeen Fishing Village
• Jewellery Factory & Repulse Bay
• Evening Symphony of Lights show at Tsim Sha Tsui

Day 3: Disneyland Adventure

• Full-day at Hong Kong Disneyland
• Enjoy themed rides, parades & fireworks
• Return to hotel in the evening

Day 4: Ocean Park or Lantau Island

• Option 1: Spend the day at Ocean Park
• Option 2: Visit Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car, Big Buddha & Po Lin Monastery
• Evening at leisure for shopping

Day 5: Transfer to Macau

• Ferry ride to Macau
• Check-in to hotel
• Visit Ruins of St. Paul’s, Senado Square, A-Ma Temple
• Explore casinos & nightlife (Venetian, Galaxy)

Day 6: Macau Sightseeing

• Visit Macau Tower (optional Bungee Jumping)
• Taipa Village & Museum
• Shopping at Cotai Strip
• Enjoy House of Dancing Water show (optional)

Day 7: Departure

• Check-out from hotel
• Transfer to Hong Kong/Macau airport for return flight
